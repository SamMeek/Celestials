The Birth of the Celestials - A Bloxfruits Crew

On the storm-lashed night of February 5th, 2025, the seas of Bloxfruits trembled—not from a raid or a devil fruit awakening, but from the rise of a name that would soon echo across all islands: The Celestials.

Formed by a fearless and enigmatic captain known only as AnimeAzelYT aka The Zenith, the crew was born from chaos, ambition, and an unshakable dream—to rise above the mortal ranks of pirates and carve their names into the stars. But even a legend needs a second heartbeat, and that heartbeat came in the form of the fiercely loyal and cunning Vice Captain ichhadari_dog aka The Zanythra.

Once a lone fighter on the outskirts of the Grand Line, Zanythra was infamous for his unmatched agility and a dogged will to survive—even when the tides turned. He had no crew, no alliances, only his instincts. But when he crossed paths with Zenith in the heat of a Sea Beast hunt, something changed. They didn't speak much—but they didn't need to. Two warriors, one vision. The sky was no longer the limit. It was their battleground.

The Celestials were not formed for mere power or riches. No—this crew was different. Each member was handpicked not for strength alone, but for soul. For resolve. For their ability to rise when all else fell. Their symbol, a radiant cosmic 'C', would wave on a star-kissed flag—each stroke representing dreams unbound by the sea.

And so, the Celestials began their journey. From raiding forgotten forts to protecting weaker crews from tyrants, they became more than just pirates. They became legends in the making. With Zanythra watching the crews back like a shadow, and Zenith carving their path forward, the world quickly realized:

The sky was no longer safe—because the Celestials had taken flight.
