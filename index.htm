The Celestials Legacy

Ascending beyond ordinary piracy to celestial dominance

Founded in the celestial tides of 2025, the Celestials emerged not just as a crew—but as a movement, a legend born beneath the starlit skies of the Grand Line. What began with a single ship and a shared dream has transformed into one of the most revered and feared forces across the entire Bloxfruits universe.

Led by the enigmatic and unshakable Zenith and Zarythra, the Celestials embody a perfect balance of wisdom, power, and vision. From the very start, their mission was clear: conquer the uncharted, master the unknown, and ascend beyond mortal limits. This was not just about treasure or territory—it was about crafting destiny among the stars.
